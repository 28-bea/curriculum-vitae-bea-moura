    flores do linho

    Logo for a women’s non-profit association that works with linen.

    The name “Flores do Linho” (line flowers) comes from the land, from the softness of women and from the growth and evolution of the process in its various stages, as well as a flower - which at the beginning is a seed, going through several stages until it blooms.

    As for the representative image of the name I wanted something simple but that didn't lose the movement and the “naturality”.
